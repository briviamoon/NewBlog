Types of Physiotherapy Available

Musculoskeletal Physiotherapy

Focused on treating conditions affecting muscles, joints, and soft tissues, including sports injuries and back pain.

Neurological Physiotherapy

Specializes in rehabilitation for patients with neurological conditions such as stroke, multiple sclerosis, or Parkinson's disease.

Cardiovascular Physiotherapy

Aims to improve cardiovascular health and recovery, particularly after heart surgery or in patients with chronic heart conditions.

Pediatric Physiotherapy

Provides therapy for children with developmental, neuromuscular, or orthopedic conditions to enhance physical development.

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Focused on the needs of older adults, addressing issues such as arthritis, osteoporosis, and mobility challenges.
